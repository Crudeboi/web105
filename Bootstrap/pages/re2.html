"Resident Evil 2" is a survival horror video game that takes place in the fictional Raccoon City. Players can choose to play as one of two characters, Leon Kennedy or Claire Redfield, each with their own unique storylines. The game begins when a zombie outbreak spreads throughout the city due to a dangerous virus created by the Umbrella Corporation. Leon and Claire find themselves trapped in the chaos and must navigate the city's dark and dangerous streets while uncovering the truth behind the outbreak. As they explore the creepy police station and other locations, players must solve puzzles, gather resources, and fight off hordes of undead creatures. The game's tension and jump scares keep players on edge as they attempt to survive and escape the horrors of Raccoon City. "Resident Evil 2" is known for its atmospheric graphics, terrifying enemies, and a feeling of constant dread. The game's story unfolds as players make choices that affect the outcome, providing replay value and different story experiences. The game is a classic in the survival horror genre, and its success has led to numerous sequels and adaptations. "Resident Evil 2" is praised for its intense and immersive gameplay, making it a must-play for fans of horror and survival games.

4.0/5.0
