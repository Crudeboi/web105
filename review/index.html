Red Dead Redemtion 2

Rockstar Games follow up to the wonderful and spectacular game that is Red Dead Redemtion. This game lays down the foundation as a prequel to the first. In Red Dead Redemtion 2, we follow Rockstar Games intricate story telling that takes the player on an emotional ride!

Good vs Evil

as we view the world thru the main protagonist Arthur Morgan, the player faces positive and negative choices that impact the conclusion of the story. The choices are yours to decide in the vast world of the Wild West. Prepare yourself for an in depth video-game unlike any other.
